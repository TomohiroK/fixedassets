Dual Book: Local Tax vs. IFRS

Maintain independent depreciation parameters for Local Tax and IFRS on each asset. Generate separate postings and switch your entire application view with a single toggle.

IFRS mode uses pure Straight-Line or Declining Balance without country-specific tax adjustments. Assets without IFRS settings are automatically skipped.

Powerful Depreciation Engine

Monthly depreciation processing with bulk, category, or individual scope. Preview before execution, double-processing prevention, and flexible rollback (current/previous month or total cancel).

Category-wise summary with time-slice capability. Select any point in time via calendar to see accumulated depreciation, book value, and progress per category.
