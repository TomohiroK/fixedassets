# Vietnam (VN) - Fixed Asset Depreciation Rules

## Currency: VND

## Fixed Asset Recognition
- Cost >= VND 30,000,000 AND useful life > 1 year

## Depreciation Methods
- **Straight-line** (most common)
- **Declining balance** (reducing balance)
- **Units of production**

## Useful Life Ranges (Circular 45/2013)

| Asset Category | Min Life | Max Life |
|----------------|----------|----------|
| Buildings & structures | 25 | 50 years |
| Machinery & equipment (general) | 5 | 10 years |
| Specialized machinery | 7 | 15 years |
| Vehicles / transport | 6 | 10 years |
| Computers & electronics | 3 | 5 years |
| Office equipment | 5 | 10 years |
| Intangible assets | -- | 20 years max |

Enterprise chooses useful life within the min-max range and notifies tax authority.

## Salvage Value
- **0** (in practice). No mandatory minimum.

## Straight-Line
```
Annual Expense = Cost / Useful Life
```

## Declining Balance
```
Annual Expense = Book Value x (SL Rate x Acceleration Factor)
```
- Acceleration factor: 1.5x (life <=4 years), 2.0x (life 5-6 years), 2.5x (life >6 years)
- Only allowed if enterprise is profitable

## Special Rules
- Useful life must be within Circular 45 prescribed min-max range
- Useful life can only be changed ONCE per asset (with justification + tax notification)
- Accelerated depreciation: up to 2x SL rate (enterprise must be profitable)
- If depreciation falls outside prescribed range, excess is non-deductible

## Category Restrictions

| Category | SL | DB | UoP | Notes |
|----------|----|----|-----|-------|
| Land | N/A | N/A | N/A | Non-depreciable |
| Buildings | Yes | Yes | No | 25-50 years |
| Machinery | Yes | Yes | Yes | 5-15 years |
| Vehicles | Yes | Yes | No | 6-10 years |
| Computers | Yes | Yes | No | 3-5 years |
| Software | Yes | No | No | Intangible, up to 20 years |
| Goodwill | Yes | No | No | Intangible, up to 20 years |
| Patents | Yes | No | No | Intangible, up to 20 years |
